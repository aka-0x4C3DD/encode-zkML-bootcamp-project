Privacy-Preserving Emotion Analysis

Analyze emotions on Bluesky posts with Fully Homomorphic Encryption for privacy

About This Project

This application allows you to analyze the emotions in Bluesky posts related to specific questions without revealing the actual posts or the emotion analysis model. It uses Fully Homomorphic Encryption to ensure privacy while maintaining the integrity of the analysis.

The emotion analysis is performed on posts fetched from the Bluesky API, and the results are presented in a user-friendly, interactive format. Fully Homomorphic Encryption (using TenSEAL) allows computations to be performed directly on encrypted data without ever decrypting it.
